CONFERENCE ANNOUNCEMENT

Tashkent University of Information Technologies named after Muhammad al-Khwarizmi is holding the International Scientific and Technical Conference “Digital Technologies: Problems and Solutions for Practical Implementation in an Industry” on April 27-28, 2022.

Conference topics

  • 1. Digital signal and image processing.
  • 2. Artificial intelligence.
  • 3. Computer systems and tools in medicine.
  • 4. Management methods in an agriculture (Precision agriculture).
  • 5. Robotics.
  • 6. Wireless and satellite communication technologies.
All conditions for the conference will be created: a personal computer, an electronic projector and the necessary software. Presentations must be prepared in Power Point. Papers must be uploaded in the relevant direction on the website conferences-list.uz until April 15, 2022. Languages: Uzbek, English and Russian

CONFERENCE PUBLICATION

Conference papers will be published in the written (original) language and in printed form. The paper (article and additional information files) is registered for publication on the conference website and uploaded to the appropriate webpage. The paper is 3-5 full pages, doc or docx. format.

PAPERS MUST BE FORMATTED BY THE TEMPLATE FILE

The text of the article will not be revised or changed. Articles are non-refundable. The author(s) are personally responsible for the accuracy of the information provided. PLEASE FOLLOW THE E-MAIL NOTIFICATIONS AFTER UPLOADING AN ARTICLE ON THE CONFERENCE WEBSITE. IF YOU RECEIVE THE LINK FOR RE-UPLOADING, YOU MUST UPLOAD YOUR ARTICLE WITH RELEVANT CORRECTIONS . Information about the inclusion of the article in the conference program will be notified to the author by e-mail before April 23, 2022. Participation in the conference is free.

The conference articles are recognized as the best articles at the conference will be published in the university journals "Vestnik TUIT" and "Muhammad al-Khorezmi avlodlari" (after fulfilling the editing requirements in accordance with the requirements of the journals).

Note: The editors reserve the right to select submitted materials. Tashkent University of Information Technologies named after Muhammad al-Khwarizmi Address: Tashkent, 100202, A.Temir street, 108. Tel.: (+998 91) 388-56-66
